Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal, a symbol of eternal love, is one of the most beautiful monuments in the world, showcasing the grandeur of Mughal architecture.

Why Visit?

Admire the beauty of white marble architecture and immerse yourself in its timeless love story.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit the Taj Mahal is between the months of November and February. The time between these months is the most pleasant and travellers can enjoy visiting the lush gardens around the monument too. Other times of the year are mostly hot and damp, and can be uncomfortable for exploration.

Architecture

The Taj Mahal seamlessly blends Persian, Islamic, and Indian architectural elements, making it a masterpiece of Mughal heritage.Although a wonder from the Mughal era, the Taj Mahal in Agra carries a fluent expression of elements inspired from the Persian, Islamic and Indian styles of architecture..

How to Reach

The Taj Mahal is just 200 km from New Delhi. Travelers can take the Yamuna Expressway by car or bus, or travel by train to Agra and hire a cab to the site.

Nearby Tourist Spots of Taj Mahal

Some other tourist spots you can explore when visiting the Taj Mahal include:

  • Agra Fort
  • Jama Masjid
  • Jahangir's Palace
  • Mankameshwar Temple
  • Chini-Ka-Rauza
  • Tomb of Itmad-Ud-Daula
  • Mehtab Bagh
